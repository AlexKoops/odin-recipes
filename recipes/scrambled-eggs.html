Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Description

Scrambled Eggs on Toast is the epitome of breakfast comfort,
offering a harmonious blend of simplicity and nourishment to start the day.
This dish features softly scrambled eggs, tender and creamy,
piled generously atop a slice of golden, crispy toast.
The contrast between the delicate, buttery eggs and the crunch
of the toast creates a satisfying texture and flavor experience.
It's a dish that appeals to a wide range of tastes, providing a warm,
comforting start to the morning.

Whether enjoyed in its purest form or customized with a sprinkle of herbs,
cheese, or a dash of hot sauce, Scrambled Eggs on Toast remains a beloved classic
that symbolizes the joy of simple pleasures and the art of cooking with care.
It embodies a versatile meal option that can be adapted to suit individual preferences,
making it a staple in kitchens around the world. The dish's popularity lies
in its ability to combine basic ingredients into something that feels
both familiar and special, inviting everyone to savor the moment with each bite.


Ingredients

  • Eggs: 2 large
  • Milk or cream: 1 tablespoon (optional, for creamier eggs)
  • Salt: to taste
  • Black pepper: to taste
  • Butter: 1 tablespoon, for cooking
  • Bread: 1-2 slices
  • Optional garnishes: chopped herbs(such as chives or parsley), grated
    cheese, or a dash of hot sauce

How to make it:

  1. Prep the ingredients: Crack 2 large eggs into a bowl. Add 1 tablespoon
    of milk or cream if desired, for creamier eggs. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Beat the
    eggs gently until the yolks and whites are just combined.

  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick skillet on the stove over low to medium
    heat. Add 1 tablespoon of butter to the pan, allowing it to melt and coat the bottom of the skillet.

  3. Cook the eggs: Pour the beaten eggs into the skillet. Let them sit,
    undisturbed, for a few seconds until they begin to set around the edges. Using a spatula, gently
    stir the eggs to flow to the bottom. Continue to cook, stirring gently, until the eggs are softly
    set and slightly runny in places. Remove from heat; the residual heat will continue to cook the eggs
    to a perfect consistency.

  4. Toast the bread: While the eggs are cooking, toast 1-2 slices of bread to
    your preferred level of crispiness, either in a toaster or in a separate pan with a little butter
    for extra flavor.

  5. Assemble the Dish: Butter the toasted bread lightly if desired. Spoon the
    scrambled eggs onto the toast, spreading them evenly.

  6. Add Garnishes (Optional): Sprinkle with chopped herbs, grated cheese, or
    adash of hot sauce to enhance the flavor.

  7. Serve: Enjoy your Scrambled Eggs on Toast immediately while everything is
    still warm and the toast is crispy.
